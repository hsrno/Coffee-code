About Us

More Than Coffee: The Hub for Innovation.

Coffee&Code was born from the synergy between caffeine and creativity. We select high quality beans to sustain long coding sessions, ensuring every sip supports your concentration and innovative drive.

Our space is designed to be the perfect meeting point whether you're pairing up for a quick sprint, brainstorming a new project, or just taking a necessary break from the screen. We believe great code starts with great connection.

At Coffee&Code, sustainability and quality go hand in hand. We partner with local farmers to source ethically grown beans, ensuring that every sip supports communities and preserves the environment. It’s our way of making sure your coffee tastes good and does good.

Our Coffee Menu

Explore blends crafted to enhance productivity and creativity.

- Espresso -

A full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in "shots."

- Americano -

Simple yet timeless espresso infused with hot water, delivering a clean, crisp taste with a lighter body.

- Cappucino -

Smooth yet inviting, espresso blended with steamed milk and crowned with airy foam, delivering a balanced flavor wrapped in warmth.

- Latte -

Gentle and comforting, espresso blended with silky steamed milk for a mellow, soothing flavor that warms the soul.

- Flat White -

A warm embrace of bold espresso and velvety microfoam, crafted for those who seek comfort in every sip.

- Mocha -

Rich and indulgent, espresso blended with chocolate and silky milk, offering a sweet harmony that delights every sip.

- Affogato -

Classic espresso poured over vanilla ice cream, combining warmth and sweetness in a delightful treat.

- Machito -

A powerful shot of espresso 'stained' with a dollop of velvety milk foam. Bold, sweet, and perfectly balanced, offering an intense yet elegant coffee experience.
